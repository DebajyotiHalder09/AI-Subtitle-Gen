About This Project

This project is a web application developed to generate and play subtitles along with your audio. If you have difficulties understanding English conversation and you want to improve by listening to audiobooks, podcasts, and you do not understand what they are talking about, this is the right place. It is designed to provide users with a seamless experience in generating subtitles powered by Open-AI's Whisper.

I have used Python, Flask, and JavaScript to build this project. The goal is to create a user-friendly interface while ensuring optimal performance.
